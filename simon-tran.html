Simon Tran

About the artist

Simon Tran is an artist working and living in Berkeley, CA. As the son of Vietnamese immigrants in the USA he pays tribute to his heritage in his paintings. Through a bold use of colour and abstract shapes and forms he redirects the cultural trauma that was caused by the war and colonialism in Vietnam. In his own words, Simon describes his work as being about reconfiguration, resistance and a hearty bowl of soup. We love it! Follow Simon Tran on Instagram to see more of his vibrant work.
