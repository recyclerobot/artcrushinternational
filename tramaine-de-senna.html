Tramaine de Senna

About the artist

Tramaine de Senna is an American artist working and living in Antwerp, Belgium. Tramaine’s body of work consists of sculptures in a wide array of colors, materials and techniques. Her interest in issues as the “migration of forms”, pop and material culture, internalized violence, exuberance and the enduring ambiguous presence of histories raise dramatic questions about identity. Follow Tramaine on Instagram to see more of her beautiful and interesting work.
