Elle Sofe Sara

About the artist

Elle Sofe Sara is a director and choreographer living and working in Guovdageaidnu, Norway. In her work she sheds light on the social, political and cultural peculiarities and challenges of the Sámi people. For more news about Elle Sofe Sara’s performances follow her on Instagram.
