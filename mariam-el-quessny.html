Mariam el-Quessny

Mariam el-Quessny invites us into her small studio in the apartment she shares with her husband and two young kids. She serves us tea and sweets and joins us in building a blanket fort.

About the artist

Mariam el-Quessny is an Egyptian artist and interior designer working and living in Oakland, CA. She’s the mom of three children and has been illustrating her and other mothers’ stories about motherhood as a reaction to the often idealised images of motherhood on social media. Follow Mariam or her Motherhood Illustrated Series on Instagram to see more of her beautiful and detailed work.
